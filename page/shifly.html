Shifly

Empowering you

Shifly is designed around empowering shift managers at McDonalds to set up their shift correctly and food safe to run a great shift.

Why Shifly?

Meet Shifly, the ultimate mobile app for Shift Managers, at McDonalds, designed to simplify your day and boost efficiency. Shifly streamlines your entire shift, starting with a user-friendly floorplan to effortlessly place your crew. It then creates a detailed, organized breaklist and a clear "Next In" screen, so you're always on top of staffing.

Shifly's innovative Travel Path guides you through the store, helping you conduct food safety checks and assign tasks to maintain a clean and safe environment. With its built-in task list, you can input your own duties, and the app will automatically display in a list. Critical daily food safety task will display at your exact location.

Stay ahead with intelligent notifications: get a 15-minute advance alert for each crew member's shift end, including their station, giving you ample time to plan coverage. You'll also receive a two-hour notification reminder to perform another food safety travel path.

Built on Kotlin Multiplatform, Shifly provides a seamless experience across all devices, from Android tablets to iPhones. But on Android devices, it truly shines by leveraging Material You to create a personalized color theme that adapts to your phone's wallpaper, making the app feel uniquely yours.
