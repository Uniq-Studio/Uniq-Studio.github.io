Shifly

Empowering you

Shifly empowers McDonald's Shift Managers to run their shifts efficiently and ensure food safety is met, enabling them to effectively manage their shift responsibilities.

Why Shifly?

Meet Shifly, the ultimate mobile app for Shift Managers, at McDonalds, designed to simplify your day and boost efficiency. Shifly streamlines your entire shift, starting with a user-friendly floorplan to effortlessly place your crew. It then creates a detailed, organized breaklist and a clear "Next In" screen, so you're always on top of staffing.

Shifly's innovative Travel Path guides you through the store, helping you conduct food safety checks and assign tasks to maintain a clean and safe environment. With its built-in task list, you can input your own duties, and the app will automatically display in a list. Critical daily food safety task will display at your exact location.

Stay ahead with intelligent notifications: get a 15-minute advance alert for each crew member's shift end, including their station, giving you ample time to plan coverage. You'll also receive a two-hour notification reminder to perform another food safety travel path.

Built on Kotlin Multiplatform, Shifly provides a seamless experience across all devices, from Android tablets to iPhones. But on Android devices, it truly shines by leveraging Material You to create a personalized color theme that adapts to your phone's wallpaper, making the app feel uniquely yours.

Always on the Ball

During your shift, there are numerous tasks and distractions to manage. Shifty will provide notifications to keep you informed about upcoming events, ensuring you stay on track.

As a crew member nearing the end of your shift, you will receive a 15-minute notification to allow you to plan your coverage. This will prevent awkward situations where you discover you have missed one crew member.

Food safety is paramount in maintaining a safe working environment. Shifty will remind you to complete another food safety check 2 hours after last completion, assisting you in identifying any potential issues promptly.

When will it expire?

During your travel path, Shifly will prompt you to input the dates of all the products in the area you are in. This ensures that the current shift manager verifies the dates and ensures accurate rotation.

Upon reviewing the dates in the food safety section, Shifly will highlight the dates that expire today, indicating to the shift manager that they should notify the next manager or take appropriate action to dispose of the products.

This feature provides Business Managers with assurance that Shift Managers are dedicating time to verify dates and can access the dates through the application to ensure accuracy with what is physically present on the store's floor.

Yes, We are Food Safe!

During your travel path, Shifly will provide you with a list of food safety tasks that you must complete in the area. Upon successful completion of these tasks, Shifly will organise the necessary actions for you, allowing you to delegate Crew or Managers to rectify any issues and ensure your store is food safe.

As you progress through your travel path, Shifly will prompt you to complete another travel path within a two-hour timeframe. This periodic review helps you identify potential lapses in food safety compliance early on, preventing them from escalating into more significant problems.

Plans are underway to shorten the interval between inspections, as busier establishments may require more frequent inspections.

Works Great with iPadOS

iPadOS's Stage Manager or an Android equivalent offers an exceptional method to harness Shifly's capabilities. By running Shifly alongside other work applications, you gain complete control over your shift and transform your tablet into a central hub for your operations.

Reparc enables you to monitor your production's times without the need to leave your workstation or depleting the till for generating reports.

Utilise Lifelenz or a similar application to view sales and labour data, while simultaneously keeping the Notes app open for capturing on-the-go notes.

Disclaimer

Shifly operates optimally on Android devices and iPads. However, due to the stringent application management policies of iOS, iPhones may unexpectedly terminate Shifly, resulting in data loss for the shift.

Although we strongly advise against using Shifly on iPhones, it is recommended to keep Shifly visible at all times to prevent iPhone from terminating Shifly. If multitasking is necessary, periodically reopen Shifly to prevent iPhone from shutting down Shifly. We recommend using only one application before returning to Shifly.

Shifly does not retain any data. Upon completing your shift, close the application, and it will automatically wipe all data, preparing it for your next shift. Additionally, Shifly does not communicate with any external servers.
