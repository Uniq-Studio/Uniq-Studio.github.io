Get Caffeinated

Uniq Coffee is a simplified coffee ordering app, created to get your coffee faster and easier.

Why Uniq Coffee?

Meet Uniq Coffee, the simplified mobile app for buying your next caffeinated drink. Uniq Coffee streamlines the coffee ordering experience, starting with setting up your Uniq ID to save your data for quicker checkout. You then can start your order and quickly get to the payment screen. After 10 purchases, you get a free coffee on your next purchase!

Uniq Coffee's interface is designed to be clean and efficient. Orders can be placed in just a few seconds, while expandable info boxes give you detailed insights about calorie content and milk options—so you always know exactly what you’re getting.

The Stamp Card is always visible on the home screen, letting you track your rewards at a glance. A progress bar encourages you to grab that next coffee and unlock your complimentary drink even sooner.

Built on Kotlin with Compose, Uniq Coffee provides a beautiful experience across all Android devices. It truly shines by leveraging Material You to create a personalized color theme that adapts to your phone's wallpaper, making the app feel uniquely yours.

Your Uniq ID

The Out-of-Box Experience begins with Uniq Coffee asking for your name and preferred payment method. This helps personalize the app for you right from the start. Your name is used to greet you on the Home Screen and to identify your coffee orders, making the experience feel more personal.

Adding a payment method during setup is optional, but you'll need one at checkout—either your saved method or Google Pay—to complete your purchase.

You can update or change your payment details anytime in the Settings menu, keeping your Uniq ID up to date.

Simplified Experience

The Uniq Coffee experience is designed to keep your focus where it belongs—on choosing the perfect coffee. The app is built to save you time, so you spend less time tapping through screens and more time enjoying your drink.

Nothing important is hidden or removed. All the information you need is either right in front of you or just a single tap away, making the process effortless.

With a clean, intuitive layout, there's no more confusing navigation. You can find what you want instantly and place your order in seconds, so your coffee is always just within reach.

Detailed Info a Tap Away

At Uniq Coffee, we prioritize a clean interface while still giving you access to all the details you might need. If you'd like more information, simply tap the expand button to reveal additional content related to what you're viewing.

For example, if you want to check the exact type of milk in your drink, just tap the drop-down to see a full description. This way, the essentials stay front and center, while the extra details are always just a tap away.

Free Coffee?

Everyone loves a good deal, and at Uniq Coffee, your loyalty is rewarded. For every 10 coffees you purchase, you'll receive a free coffee voucher—redeemable on any drink of your choice.

Best of all, these vouchers never expire, so you can enjoy your reward whenever it suits you. Simply apply it at the payment screen, and your free drink will be prepared just for you.

Let's be honest—free coffee has never tasted better.
