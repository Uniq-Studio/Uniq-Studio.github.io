My City

Best of Glasgow

My City - Glasgow provides users with a guide to the city of Glasgow. By selecting an area of interest, users can access a curated list of recommended destinations.

Why My City?

Meet My City, the simple mobile app that helps you discover the best spots in Glasgow. Whether you're looking for a new café, restaurant, or park, My City makes exploring easy. Just choose what you want to do, and you'll get a curated list of places to visit—helping you uncover your next favourite spot in the city.

The app's interface is clean and efficient, designed to get you to what you want quickly. My City highlights five key interests: Coffee Shops, Restaurants, Kid-Friendly Places, Parks, and Shopping Centres—giving you plenty of options to explore.

Each location includes a detailed panel with everything you need to know: services offered, accepted payment types, price range, and exact location. That way, you can try new places with confidence—no surprises, just great experiences.

Built on Kotlin with Compose, My City provides a beautiful experience across all Android devices. It truly shines by leveraging Material You to create a personalized color theme that adapts to your phone's wallpaper, making the app feel uniquely yours. It adapts to screen sizes so the app leverages the screen's real estate it has to play with.

Simplicity is key

The app's simplified layout gives you everything you need—without overwhelming ads, suggestions, or promoted locations. All the important details are right at your fingertips, so you can find what matters and get there faster.

You'll know in advance how expensive a location is and which payment types are accepted, so there's no stress when it comes to paying. My City keeps the experience clear, simple, and worry-free.

Straight to the point

My City's experience is designed to keep your focus where it belongs—on choosing the fun task. The app is built to save you time, so you spend less time tapping through screens and more time enjoying your time in Glasgow.

Nothing important is hidden. All the information you need is right in front of you, just a tap away—never buried under distractions.

With a clean, intuitive layout, there's no more confusing navigation. You can find what you want instantly.

Adapts to all screen sizes

My City will adapt to your screen size, ensuring that the app displays beautifully on any device.

The app will dynamically update its layout, optimising the use of extra screen real estate when utilising a foldable device.

My City is optimised for all device types, including mobile, foldable, and tablets.

Disclaimer

This app is intended for personal use and entertainment purposes only. It does not connect to any external servers, and all data is stored locally on your device. Therefore information about places may be out of date.

Information you view may be inaccurate, please do research about the location you would like to visit.

This app is a personal project and it is not intended for commercial use. It uses resources from various sources for educational and experimental purposes.
