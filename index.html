About Us

Welcome to Uniq Studio, where simplicity meets innovation! Founded in 2020, we are passionate about software development within mobile applications that's designed to enhance your tasks. Our journey began in with a vision of practical need; simplifying shift running as a Shift Manager at McDonald's UK. This created the core of Uniq; to design app to convert complex tasks to straightforward process, allowing the user to focus on what truly matters.

Our applications are built with the purpose of serving helpful reminders and guides to tasks and make all the information readily accessible. We are committed to creating uniform apps with a seamless design between all devices. We use Google Android's Compose for the design and development and utilizing Kotlin Multiplatform, to bring it to all devices and having that 'Uniq' look to it.
