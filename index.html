About Us

Welcome to Uniq Studio, where simplicity meets innovation! Founded in 2020, we are passionate about software development within mobile applications that's designed to enhance your tasks. Our journey began in with a vision of practical need; simplifying shift running as a Shift Manager at McDonald's UK. This created the core of Uniq; to design app to convert complex tasks to straightforward process, allowing the user to focus on what truly matters.

Our applications are built with the purpose of serving helpful reminders and guides to tasks and make all the information readily accessible. We are committed to creating uniform apps with a seamless design between all devices, which we use Google Android's Compose for the design and development and utilizing Kotlin Multiplatform, to bring it to all devices and having that 'Uniq' look to it.

Who We Are

Uniq Studio is a solo endeavor, led by me, Aaron. This is a platform designed around building and learning, with refining your skills. Using skills I learnt to build amazing apps. My vision for Uniq Studio is to extend the team in the future, the growth helping us develop and deliver projects faster and have a will-ploshied design, enhancing our apps.

Our Mission

At Uniq Studio, our mission is clear: to simplify life through innovative apps. We strive to create fundamental applications that streamline everyday tasks, guiding users effortlessly through their responsibilities. More than just a development house, Uniq Studio is a hub for continuous learning and growth. It's a testament to how practical application can showcase and solidify everything learned in the ever-evolving world of technology.
