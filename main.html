About Us

Welcome to Uniq Studio, where simplicity meats innovation! Founded in 2020, we are passionate about software development within mobile applications thats designed to enhace your tasks. Our journey began in with a vision of practical need; simplifying shift running as a Shift Manager at McDonald's UK. This created the core of Uniq; to design app to convert complext tasks to straightforward process, allowing the user to focus on what truly matters.

Our applications are built with the purpose of serving helpful reminders and guides to tasks and make all the information readily accessable. We are committed to creating uniform apps with a seemless design between all devices, which we use Google Android's Compose for the design and development and utilizing Kotlin Multiplatform, to bring it to all devices and having that 'Uniq' look to it.
