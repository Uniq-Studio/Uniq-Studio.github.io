Kick-Start

Streamlined Access for Modern Workflows.

Kick-Start empowers the modern workforce by centralizing priority applications into a single, intuitive interface. It ensures seamless navigation and enabling users to maintain focus throughout their day.

Why Kick-Start?

Kick-Start is more than just a launcher; it is a dedicated environment designed to minimize friction and maximize focus. By centralizing your most important tools into one high-performance interface, Kick-Start removes the clutter of standard Android home screens.

Built with Jetpack Compose and Material 3, it provides a tactile, modern experience that adapts to your needs. Whether you are managing a busy retail floor or a professional workspace, Kick-Start ensures that your priority apps are always exactly where you need them.

Simplicity is Key

Kick-Start launcher strikes a perfect balance of aesthetics elements, while keeping functional discipline. Featuring smooth animations and premium 'Uniq' backdrop.

By curating a limited selection of essential apps. Kickstart removes all potential apps that lead to distractions, keeping the most critical tools the primary focus.

Removing the overcrowded feel helps users stay on track and launch the app they require next.

Protected

Kick-Start is designed with a focus on operational integrity, ensuring that sensitive configurations and support resources remain out of sight for standard user. The PIN-entry system acts like a protective barrier, designed to prevent unauthorized use of the system.

This helps ensure that only authorized personnel can modify system parameters or to update configurations and update the system app and operating system. Preventing accidental changes that could disrupt workflow.

While this barrier provides basic protection for daily operations, it is important to keep in mind this is a basic utility and not a deep system protection. The PIN is stored locally on device for quick easy offline access.

Easy Access to Support

About app screen has dedicated links to help new users understand their new environment. Kick-Start provides immediate access to critical sections

Kick-Start provides access to a Getting Started guide, and the Privacy Policy and Terms of Service, ensuring that the users are never left without the essentials.

To maintain the focus experience, all support resources are loaded directly from Uniq Studio's website within the apps internal browser. This helps keep the user journey unified, allowing for quick review over documentation, without leaving the workspace.

Material You

Kick-Start is built upon the Material 3 design language, ensuring intuitive experience that feels modern across all devices.

A core highlight is the integration of Dynamic Color, the app will dynamically update the color scheme from your device's wallpaper making it feel uniquely yours.

Dynamic Color has been disabled on the launcher screen to keep with the 'Uniq' theming alongside the built-in wallpaper, keeping the launcher feeling unified across devices.
